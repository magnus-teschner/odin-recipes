Apple Cake Recipe

Return to main page

Description

Indulge in a delightful Apple Cake, a perfect blend of warm spices and juicy apples. This cake is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups apples, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Beat in the flour mixture alternately with the milk, mixing just until incorporated. Fold in the chopped apples and nuts (if using).
  5. Pour batter into the prepared pan and spread it out evenly.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow to cool before serving. Enjoy your delicious Apple Cake!
